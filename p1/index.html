Nergigante


Name Nergigante
Species Elder Dragon
Elemental Affinity None
Elemental Weakness Thunder

Let's start with a big one first, Nergigante. Affectionately named Nergi Boi by my group of friends
I hunt with, his quick lashing attacks, devistating divebombs and near impenetrable spikes will cause trouble
for even seasoned hunter.

The Fight

Nergigante is fast and ruthless, usually favoring long lunging attacked and big dive bombs to overwhelm you.
Always keep an eye on who he's focusing on because he may suprise an unattentive hunter with one of his
long distance dive bombs. Along with his devestating power is Nergigante's spikes that he grows on his body.
The will start white and slowly grow from his arms and wings and after enough time they will become black
and impenetrable to your weapons

The Strategy

While Nergigante is formibale, he's not unbeatable, here's a few tips to help in the fight:

  • Have one of your hunters act as support, keeping an eye on everyones health and applying buffs when needed
  • Attack his spikes, they're weak points and do extra damage, also if you damage them enough, they will break
    and has to start the growing process again
  • When he flies up high, get ready to dodge or defend, any weapon with a heavy shield and the proper buffs can
    survive his dive bombs, but your best bet is to run
  • Keep your hunters spread out, focuse on different parts of his body to weaken him more effectively, also you
    don't want to be close together when he does his dive bombs, multiple hunters going down will end
    your hunt very quickly

Kirin


Name Kirin
Species Elder Dragon
Elemental Affinity Thunder
Elemental Weakness Fire

Next up is another Elder Dragon by the name of Kirin, or as I like to call it, Lightning Pony. This monster
will utilize the incredible power of lightning to dissorient it foes and keep them away. Kirin is also very
mobile, making it very difficult for hunters to keep up with it.

The Fight

Kirin will do her best to keep away from the hunters, often favoring wide area attacks and jumping around to avoid
being hit by hunters. In the Coral Highlands where you fight her, she'll use the multiple levels present on the stage to
her advantage to quickly bound away from the fight. What would take the hunter minutes to scale, she can jump in a
few moments. When she does unleash her lightning, it'll be a storm of random strikes around her, spread out area
blasts and a focuses beam that'll roast any hunter caught in it.

The Strategy

Kirin may be fast, but a quickwitted hunter can be faster with these tips:

  • Kirin isn't the only one that can use the enviroment to her advantage, your clutch claw can latch on to the glowing
    insects hanging near the cliffs to help you scale the Coral Highlands as fast as Kirin
  • Don't bother with trying to use traps, as an Elder Dragon, these creatures are unaffected by pitfall or shock traps
  • Use status effects to your advantage such as sleep to get Kirin to fall asleep so you can set up a charge hit or bombs
    for massive damage, also poison can help inflict some slow damage over time, but any little bit helps
  • If you can corner Kirin, stay spread out, her lightning attacks can stun you and another hunter can help you recover
    faster should you be stunned, also her focused blast attack is very deadly and staying spread
    out will increase your team's survivablity

Deviljho


Name Deviljho
Species Brute Wyvern
Elemental Affinity Dragon
Elemental Weakness Dragon

Lastly we have the world eater, Deviljho otherwise know as The Pickle this beast is an alpha amongst monster. It can
survive anywhere and it's diet consists of other monsters, but it's not uncommon for this brute to snack on a hunter or
two should they get in its way

The Fight

Deviljho is a force to be reckoned with. Don't be fooled by its size, this creature is fast and will take you down before you know
it. Its breath attack is slow, but wide and if hit by it will induce the dragonblight and defense down ailments on unwarry hunters.
Dragonblight will nullify any elemental advantage you're trying to leverage on Deviljho until it subsides. Deviljho also does wide
area sweep attacks with its tail and wide chomps with its huge mouth, both of which are fast and unpredictable.

The Strategy

Deviljho may be intimidating, but these tips can help hunters chop this pickle:

  • Since Deviljho is a regular Brute Wyvern, traps are effective against him, plan where you place them and try to lead him into
    pitfalls and shocks to help give you some good hits into him
  • Be aware of his Dragonblight breath, even while he's trapped he can still use it, try to stay near his back with more mobile
    hunters braving his face
  • Use Dragon element weapons against him, they're hard to get, but they pack a ton of raw damage and the extra Dragon element
    damage is a welcome aid in your fight
  • Work on his tail, if you can severe his tail, that's one less thing to worry about when fighting him and can really turn the tide
